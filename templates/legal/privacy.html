Redditch Hockey Club
Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Redditch Hockey Club (“the Club”) collects, stores, and uses personal information provided through the Registration Portal. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

When you register, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name, date of birth, and gender
  • Contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Emergency contact details
  • Medical information relevant to safe participation in hockey
  • Membership status (senior, junior, player type)
  • Parental/guardian details for players under 18
3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • Managing club membership records
  • Communicating important club information
  • Organising training sessions, matches, and competitions
  • Ensuring the safety and wellbeing of all players
  • Meeting reporting requirements of hockey governing bodies
4. Sharing of Information

Your information will only be shared with:

  • Club coaches and captains for training, selection, and match administration
  • Relevant hockey governing bodies (e.g. England Hockey) where required
  • Medical professionals in the event of an emergency

We will never sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

Your personal data is stored securely within the Registration Portal and protected by appropriate technical and organisational measures. Access is restricted based on role (player, parent/guardian, captain, coach, administrator).

6. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as you are a member of the Club. After membership ends, data may be retained for a maximum of 3 years unless required longer by law or safeguarding regulations.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the Club Committee:
info@redditchhc.co.uk
